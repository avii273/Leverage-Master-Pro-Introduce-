About App Features

Key trading concepts made simple and actionable

What is Capital?

Capital is your trading platform or account main balance - the foundation of your trading activities.

What is Margin?

Margin is the amount required to open a trade. We recommend using no more than 5% of your capital per trade.

What is Risk?

Risk represents your maximum loss amount if a trade hits StopLoss. Manage it as money you're prepared to lose.

StopLoss Percentage

The percentage difference between your Entry Price and StopLoss price. Set it visually for precision.
